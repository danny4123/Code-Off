DESSERTS

1

Andy's Frozen Custard

Walk time: 10 minutes

No matter the time or day, you can find a cherub enjoying frozen custard at Andy’s. It’s the Cherubs go-to for Saturday late-night bites, one of the perks of studying at Northwestern! Many cherubs prefer Andy’s custard to ice cream because it’s richer and creamier. Alongside classic chocolate and vanilla, Andy’s also serves special flavors such as the Straw-Ana Sundae, Ozark Turtle, and the Triple Chocolate Concrete.

“I like Andy’s because it’s a good meeting place where everyone can hang out and get relatively cheap ice cream. I think it tastes better than anything where I live.” - Olivia Cohen, Chapel Hill, North Carolina

2

Insomnia Cookies

Cherubs love heading to Insomnia before or after check-in, or ordering in for a late night snack. Known for its late night deliveries until 3 am, Insomnia Cookies satisfies any sweet tooth at nearly any time of day. Their menu includes chocolate chip, M&M, snickerdoodle, and peanut butter chip. Cherubs also often opt for a cookiewich, which includes a generous scoop of ice cream sandwiched between two cookies of your choice.

“I really like the double chocolate chunk. I like that the cookies are fresh, but I can definitely see that it can be pretty irritating that they take a while to bake and get delivered. I think, overall, if you’re willing to wait, it’s worth it.” - Annie Zavitz, Missoula, Montana

Walk time: 10 minutes

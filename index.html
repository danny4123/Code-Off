DESSERTS

1

Andy's Frozen Custard

Walk time: 10 minutes

Andy’s is the largest dessert-only franchise in the world. Andy’s famous frozen custard, which is creamier than ice cream and freshly produced by the hour, has been served since 1986. Alongside the classic chocolate and vanilla favorites, Andy’s also serves specialty options such as the Straw-Ana Sundae, Ozark Turtle, and the Triple Chocolate Concrete. The Evanston Andy’s location is the Cherubs go-to for Saturday late-night bites.

“I like Andy’s because it’s a good meeting place where everyone can hang out and get relatively cheap ice cream. I think it tastes better than anything where I live.” - Olivia Cohen, Chapel Hill
